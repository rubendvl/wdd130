Overview

Purpose

Establishing an online presence for our water rafting company is crucial for connecting with adventure enthusiasts searching for exhilarating outdoor experiences. It allows us to engage directly with both new and returning guests, providing them with comprehensive information about our rafting tours, safety protocols, and booking options. By showcasing our expertise, commitment to safety, and customer satisfaction through engaging content and authentic testimonials, we aim to build trust and credibility in the adventure tourism industry.

Audience

Our water rafting website targets adventure-seeking individuals aged 18-45, primarily from urban areas with disposable income and a passion for outdoor activities. They are likely to be professionals or students looking to escape the city and immerse themselves in nature for weekend getaways or vacation adventures. 1: What do they want? Our target audience wants thrilling outdoor experiences that provide excitement and a break from routine. They seek activities like water rafting to challenge themselves physically and mentally. 2: What do they expect to find on the website? They expect to find detailed information about our rafting tours, including river routes, difficulty levels, safety measures, and equipment provided. 3: How will my audience access my site? The majority of our audience will access the website through their mobile devices, as they often plan their trips spontaneously or while on the move.
